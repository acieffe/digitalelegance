I'm Getting Married

On Leap Day (February 29), 2020, I proposed to my then girlfriend of three years. This application allows my to calculate the days remaining from the current date. It also calculates an entered cost to the wedding and then divides that cost over the remaining days.

Calculations & Data

  • todaysDate = Date() (standard function that retreives today's date)
  • weddingDate = User defined future date
  • weddingCost = User defined cost of the wedding
  • countdown = Calculated by subtracting today's date from the future date supplied by the user
  • savings = Calculated by dividing the user defined cost by the countdown calculated number

Output

Countdown is displayed in the form of days remaining and savings is displayed in a dollar form per day until the wedding day.
